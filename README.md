I am part of a joint training program (联合培养) at the [Yangtze Delta Region Institute (Quzhou)](http://ydri.uestc.edu.cn), [University of Electronic Science and Technology of China](https://www.uestc.edu.cn) (电子科技大学，长三角研究院).

I currently conduct research in the [Malab](http://123.57.240.48/forum.php?mod=viewthread&tid=8672) laboratory 🔬 under the supervision of [Prof. Dr. Quan Zou](http://lab.malab.cn/~zq/) (邹权教授). My main research areas are machine learning 🤖 and computational biology 🧬, with several publications in SCI-indexed journals.
